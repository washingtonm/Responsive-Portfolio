About Me

Washington Mashingaidze is a native of Zimbabwe. He immigrated to the United States as an International Student seeking to further his education. Washington graduated from Wiley College with a double major in Education and Mathematics with a minor in Computer Science. He attended the University of Connecticut (Storrs, CT) where he graduated with a Master of Science in Applied Mathematics.

Washington is visionary leader and entrepreneur in the field of science and technology who has achieved excellence in areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science and Business Analytics. He has worked in leadership roles at various Fortune 500 organizations such IBM and AIG. At both companies, he built artificial intelligence applications designed to paint employee personality portraits, identify key traits of successful leaders and ultimately provide smarter job recommendations to the organization. He also has experience in operations and finance positions.
